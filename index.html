Welcome

Lila is a little girl who has experienced a sudden and heartbreaking loss.

The loss has been so intense that she has lost her ability to speak.

Travel with Lila through her dreams and help her to find the words she needs to express how she's feeling.

During your journey, you will see clues that will help you figure out what emotions she is experiencing. Don't be afraid to use a thesaurus to help you!
